WHAT SERVICES WE PROVIDE


SOLID ARTS


Solid arts refer to the lines of type that do not contain space between each of the lines. This term is often used in quilting, where solid arts quilts are made with solid colors, without any patterns or prints.


OIL PAINTS


Oil paint is a type of slow-drying paint that consists of particles of pigment suspended in a drying oil, commonly linseed oil. The viscosity of the paint can be modified by the addition of a solvent such as turpentine or white spirit, and varnish may be added to increase the glossiness of the dried oil paint film.


GRAPHIC DESIGN


Graphic design is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content. It involves communicating ideas in a visual format, guiding perception, and informing the audience. Graphic designers work to create visuals that convey specific messages, which can range from simple business logos to complex page layouts on websites.


RETOUCH DESIGN


Retouch design, also known as photo manipulation, is a powerful tool in graphic design that involves the creative and skillful alteration of images to achieve specific visual objectives. Designers use various techniques like retouching, compositing, color correction, and digital effects to transform images.

Color Grade

Color grading is a crucial aspect of content creation, including film, photography, and graphic design. It involves adjusting the color and tone of an image or video to achieve a specific aesthetic or mood. Color grading can be used to enhance the emotional impact of a scene, create a consistent visual style, or simply to correct color imbalances.
